Welcome to dawg.city ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at dawg.city.

By using dawg.city, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

dawg.city is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the tool.

URLs you submit: When you paste a YouTube URL for analysis, that URL is sent to our backend server and forwarded to the Sightengine API for processing. We do not store submitted URLs or associate them with your identity.

dawg.city only accepts publicly available video links from supported platforms (YouTube, TikTok, X/Twitter, and Instagram). We do not process private, unlisted, or non-public content. The submitted public URL is forwarded to Sightengine solely for real-time thumbnail analysis and is discarded immediately after the request completes.

Usage data: Like most websites, we may collect basic usage data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This data is collected anonymously and used only for improving the service.
