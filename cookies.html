Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and give website owners useful information about how their site is being used.

Cookies do not typically contain any information that personally identifies you, but personal data that we store about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.

2. How We Use Cookies

dawg.city uses cookies for the following purposes:

✅ Essential Cookies
Required for the website to function properly. These include cookies that remember your cookie consent preference. You cannot opt out of these cookies.
📊 Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how visitors interact with the website by collecting and reporting anonymous usage data. Used to improve the service.
📢 Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)
Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. These cookies enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on your interests.
🔒 Security Cookies (Google reCAPTCHA)
Google reCAPTCHA v3 uses cookies to distinguish human users from bots, protecting the service from abuse. No visible challenge is shown to users.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies on your device:

  • Google AdSense — serves personalized or non-personalized advertising. Learn more
  • Google reCAPTCHA — bot detection and security. Learn more
  • Sightengine — AI content analysis API. No cookies are set directly by Sightengine in your browser.

We do not control the cookies set by these third parties. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

4. Specific Cookies We Use

  • cookie_consent — Stores your cookie consent decision (accepted/declined). Duration: 1 year. First-party.
  • _ga, _gid — Google Analytics cookies for tracking site usage anonymously. Duration: up to 2 years.
  • NID, IDE, DSID — Google advertising cookies used by AdSense. Duration: varies.
  • rc::* — Google reCAPTCHA cookies for bot detection. Session-based.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Check your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Ad settings: Opt out of personalized Google ads at google.com/settings/ads.
  • Cookie consent: When you first visit dawg.city, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies via our cookie banner.

Note: disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website, including the ability to display ads and use security features.

6. Your Consent

By clicking "Accept All" on our cookie banner, you consent to our use of all cookies described in this policy. If you click "Decline", only essential cookies will be used.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and revisiting the site.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of the page when we do so.
